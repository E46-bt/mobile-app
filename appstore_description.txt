BMW Connect is the companion app for the BMW Connect audio system — a custom DSP daemon that upgrades the factory sound setup in a BMW E46 with modern digital signal processing.

Pair over Bluetooth Low Energy or connect over Wi-Fi to control your car's audio in real time:

• Switch instantly between AirPlay and Bluetooth audio sources
• Fine-tune sound with a 10-band graphic equalizer (±12 dB per band)
• Built-in EQ presets: Flat, Bass, Treble, V-Shape, and Rock
• Adjust volume and watch live VU meters
• Toggle loudness compensation and a limiter to prevent clipping
• Mute audio with a single tap
• Monitor connection status and live daemon stats at a glance
• Dual connectivity: BLE for quick pairing, Wi-Fi for a persistent link

BMW Connect requires the companion BMW Connect daemon running on compatible hardware installed in the vehicle. It is not a standalone audio player.
